Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

FrameHand ("the app") is developed by Eric James Strawn. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

FrameHand does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely on your device.

Camera and Microphone

FrameHand requires access to your iPhone's camera and microphone to provide its core functionality — real-time subject tracking and video recording. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Photos Library

FrameHand saves recorded videos to your iPhone's Photos library. This is the only data the app writes to your device, and it is under your full control at all times.

No Third-Party Services

FrameHand does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

No Account Required

FrameHand does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Children's Privacy

FrameHand does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact
