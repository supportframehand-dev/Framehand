FrameHand

AI-powered camera operator for your iPhone

FrameHand keeps you in frame so you can focus on your content. It moves with you when you need an extra hand.

No crew. No tripod adjustments. Just set your phone down and start creating. The hands-free framing experience, brought to iPhone.

Features

  • Real-time face and body tracking powered by Apple's Vision framework
  • Intelligent crop-and-pan viewport that follows your movement
  • One-tap recording with the crop baked directly into the saved video
  • Three-finger tap to start and stop recording — perfect for mounted rigs or when the screen is partially blocked
  • Countdown timer trigger — set it and walk into frame
  • Works entirely on-device, no internet connection required
  • Saves directly to your Photos library
